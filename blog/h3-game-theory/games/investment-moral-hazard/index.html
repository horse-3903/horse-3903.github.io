Setup

Definition:

Investment Game with Moral Hazard

  • Players: Player 1 is the Investor; Player 2 is the Friend.
  • Strategies: Each period, the Investor chooses Invest or Safe; if investment occurs, the Friend chooses Repay or Abscond.
  • Rules: The game repeats infinitely. Future payoffs are discounted by δ=11+r\delta=\frac{1}{1+r}δ=1+r1​.

Payoff Details

  • If the Investor invests 100100100, total output is 130130130.
  • If the Friend repays 120120120, payoffs are (120,10)(120,10)(120,10).
  • If the Friend absconds with 130130130, payoffs are (0,130)(0,130)(0,130).
  • If the Investor chooses the safe outside option, payoffs are (100(1+r),0)(100(1+r),0)(100(1+r),0).

Derivation (Grim Trigger and One-Shot Deviations)

Grim trigger strategies

  • Investor: invest in period 000 and continue investing after histories with only Invest, Repay; after any deviation, choose Safe forever.
  • Friend: repay after every investment as long as all previous investments were repaid; after any deviation, abscond if investment is ever offered.
  • On the equilibrium path, play is Invest, Repay forever.
  • After a deviation, the punishment path is Safe forever, giving payoffs (100(1+r),0)(100(1+r),0)(100(1+r),0) each period.

Punishment-state deviations

  • In the punishment state, the Investor receives 100(1+r)100(1+r)100(1+r) forever by choosing Safe.
  • A one-shot investment during punishment gives at most 000 immediately if the Friend absconds, so it is not profitable.
  • The Friend receives 000 when no investment occurs and has no profitable one-shot deviation on the punishment path.

Nash Equilibrium

Result:

By the one-shot deviation principle, grim trigger is a subgame perfect Nash equilibrium if

r≤0.2r\leq 0.2r≤0.2

and

r≤112r\leq \frac{1}{12}r≤121​

The binding constraint is the Friend’s no-deviation condition, since

112<0.2\frac{1}{12}<0.2121​<0.2

Therefore the final equilibrium condition is

r≤112\boxed{r\leq \frac{1}{12}}r≤121​​

Social Optimum

  • Perpetual investment and repayment generates total surplus 130130130 each period.
  • The safe outside option generates total surplus 100(1+r)100(1+r)100(1+r) each period.

Insights

Insight:

The investment relationship fails first because of the Friend’s moral hazard, not because of the Investor’s outside option. A higher interest rate lowers patience and makes the future relationship less able to discipline absconding.
